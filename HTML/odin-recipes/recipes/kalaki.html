Kalaki

HOME

The egg Kalaki is a favourite in Tamil Nadu and all the more interesting with its runny and fluffy half made omelette style.

Ingredients:

  • Eggs
  • Table salt
  • Kolambu (if not plain kalaki)
  • pepper

Steps:

  1. Whisking the eggs vigorously until they are homogenous
  2. Whisk in the salt and pepper
  3. Apply oil or cheese and add whisked egg in the pan
  4. Take it out before it gets boiled
  5. Serve
