This chicken kebab recipe uses a flavorful, yogurt-based marinade. I'd like to think that all the chicken coming off American grills this summer will be as tasty, juicy, and tender as these. Even inexperienced grillers can produce impressive results! It's loosely based on a lamb marinade I've used for a long time.

Ingredients:

  • Lemon
  • Boneless Chicken
  • black pepper
  • metal skewer

Steps:

  1. Place chicken thigh halves into the yogurt marinade and coat them thoroughly on all sides
  2. Cover the bowl with plastic wrap and refrigerate, 2 to 8 hours.
  3. Preheat an outdoor grill for medium-high heat and lightly oil the grate.
  4. Place kebabs on the preheated grill. Do not try to turn them until they begin to unstick from the grill, 3 or 4 minutes
  5. Serve
